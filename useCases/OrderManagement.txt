1) The boss opens the website

2) The boss sees a stack of online orders besides which there are three buttons - "Accept", "Reject" and "View Order"

3) On Clicking the view order the boss sees the details of the order

4) The boss can either accept or reject the order

5) If the boss accepts the order, the order is marked approved and he is sent back to the home page

6) If the boss rejects the order, the order is marked rejected and he is sent back to the home page

7) If none of the buttons are clicked then item remains on the stack
